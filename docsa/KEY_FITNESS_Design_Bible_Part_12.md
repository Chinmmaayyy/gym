# KEY FITNESS --- Website Design Bible

# Part 12 --- Complete Copywriting Bible

## Brand Voice

Every sentence should sound:

-   Confident
-   Motivating
-   Professional
-   Premium
-   Friendly

Avoid exaggerated claims or generic marketing clichés.

# Copywriting Rules

-   Keep paragraphs under four lines.
-   Focus on benefits instead of features.
-   Use active voice.
-   Encourage action with every major section.
-   Maintain a premium, trustworthy tone throughout the website.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Final Goal

Every headline, paragraph, and CTA should motivate visitors to take the
next step toward joining Key Fitness while reinforcing the brand's
premium identity.
